Starr Hill Presents is an independently owned and operated concert promoter based in Charlottesville, Virginia promoting over 300 events each year, ranging from small club shows to multi-day festivals. The company operates multiple local venues and is a partner in various national music festivals including Bonnaroo, Outside Lands and SXSW.
